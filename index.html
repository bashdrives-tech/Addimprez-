Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for your project planning.

How long does it take to manufacture and install a signboard?
Depending on the size and materials, most standard LED and ACP signboards take about 3 to 7 working days from the final design approval to complete installation.
Do you handle the installation at my shop?
Yes, we provide complete, turnkey services. Our team handles the secure mounting, wiring, and final finishing at your location so it is ready to use immediately.
Which type of board is best for outdoor sunlight and rain?
We highly recommend Aluminum Composite Panel (ACP) boards combined with waterproof LED modules and acrylic lettering. They are rust-proof, highly weather-resistant, and keep their color for years.
Do you help with the design, or do I need to provide it?
We do both! If you already have a logo and design, we can print and fabricate it perfectly. If you are starting from scratch, our professional design team can create the perfect layout for your brand.
