Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for your branding and project execution.

How long does it take to manufacture and install a signboard?
Depending on the architectural scale and specific material selections, most standard premium LED and ACP signboards take around 3 to 7 working days from final design approval to deployment.
Do you handle the actual site installation?
Yes, we provide completely turnkey execution. Our technical installation team manages the structural mounting, hardware integration, wiring setups, and final architectural finishing directly at your shop front.
Which type of board is best for outdoor sunlight and heavy rain?
We highly recommend Aluminum Composite Panel (ACP) backing elements combined with fully waterproofed outdoor-rated LED modules and acrylic face letters. They are rust-proof, weather-resistant, and retain visual density for years.
